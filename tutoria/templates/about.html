Our Missions
Teaching isn't simple. It is not only related to every student's academic performance, but can also change a student's life. Tutors' responsibilities do not stop with the end of class; however, teaching is a constant process. Tutoria aims to help tutors and students to achieve their best, and we offer you the following options:
Increased exposure of tutors: students in need can easily find tutors online.
Automatic notifications and for you and your beloved students: No one misses lessons.
Friendly reminders: rescheduling and cancelling from admin notify tutors and students about the changes.
Mobile electronic devices friendly: Tutors and students schedule sessions and modify information via their mobile devices at any time.
Instant messaging: Chat with your tutors or students online!
